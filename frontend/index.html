Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about renting and listing on RentHub.

All owners go through an identity verification process before their listings go live. This includes government ID verification, profile review, and an initial quality check on their listed equipment descriptions.
A refundable security deposit is collected at booking time. In the event of damage, the owner can submit a damage claim and our support team will review evidence from both parties to determine a fair resolution. Standard wear and tear is never charged.
Yes. You can cancel a booking from your dashboard before the rental start date. Cancellation policy varies by owner — some offer full refunds, others have partial refund windows. Always check the listing's cancellation terms before booking.
