How RentHub Works

Safe, reliable peer-to-peer equipment sharing in your neighborhood.

1. Search & Filter Gear

Browse listings in our catalog. Filter results by categories (cameras, drones, power tools), maximum daily price constraints, condition ratings, and locations. Read feedback profiles left by prior renters.

2. Conflict-Free Checkout Preview

Specify your rental window timeline dates. Our backend checking service calculates pricing in real time, validating any available promo coupon codes and verifying that there are no blocked availability overlaps.

3. Accept & Check In

Once approved by the owner, a PDF checkout receipt containing verification codes is generated. Coordinate collection pickup directly with the owner, confirm equipment condition status, and activate your rental period.
